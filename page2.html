In order for dogs to thrive, they require a few basic necessities. Dogs must have daily exercise and an opportunity to go to the bathroom. Many people utilize walks in order to give their dogs both of these pivotal things. In order to take your dog on a walk, you will need a leash and a collar. Dogs also need a place to rest.
